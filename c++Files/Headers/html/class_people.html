Detailed Description

class for representing people in the map that have to be collected

People are round objects with green background that need to be collected before exiting the map. People have a unique identifier given by a digit. This identifier is their name. People inherit all shape characteristics of Circles, because their have circular shapes. In addition to their name and form people have a confidence level assigned to them indicating how confident the character recognition algorithm was when detecting their name.
